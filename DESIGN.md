I want to make the Finviz market map into a classic world of warcraft raid frame for healers minigame.

Instead of every box only being a shade or red or green, I want each box to be like a healthbar, so it's vertically full or partly full, and it's shade is determined by how empty it is (someone who's "almost dead" will be bright red).

The gameplay loop is the user clicks on a box to heal it, you see a "cast bar" going from left to right under the market map, when the cast bar fills up you "heal" the stock by a %.

You can choose either "flash heal" (faster, heals the stock a smaller amount) or "greater heal" (slower, heals a large amount).

Your "mana bar" should also be prominent, and the two spells use different amounts of mana. Except instead of "mana" it should be a "Margin bar".

The stocks should take random damage, but there should be a semi predictable pattern:

- Each group (e.g. "Technology" should be singled out for damage (primary group target) in a 10-20s period
- Sometimes all stocks are damaged ("splash damage"), sometimes one is focused and takes several big hits
- Every now and then, a stock from outside the primary group target should take some random damage, usually not that much
- If any stock reaches zero health, it becomes inactive and empty
- If all stocks hit zero health, game over
